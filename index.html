Sri Lankan Football Organisation UK

The Sri Lankan Football Organisation UK's (SLFOUK) aim is to organise competitions and tournaments in the UK to raise funds to develop and nurture football among the youth in rural or deprived areas of Sri Lanka.

SLFO is a non profit organisation to encourage and promote football in the UK and Sri Lanka within the Sri Lankan community.

SLFO will endeavour to establish good relationships between the Football Federation of Sri Lanka and the other governing bodies both in the UK and Sri Lanka to promote the welfare of football.
